---Updates---
Version 1.0:
First release of COC N BALLZ.
Version 1.1:
1 · Added walls that prevent zombies from walking through.
2 · Fixed a bug where zombies could attack you without touching you.
3 · Added keybinds for healing,trading and regenerating mana.
Version 1.1.1:
1 · Minor bug fixes
Version 1.1.2:
1 · Fixed critical lag issues during rounds 5 and above.
2 · The patch notes button now leads to this webpage.
Version 1.1.3:
1 · Walls now 'snap' to grid.
2 · There is now a 10 second delay in between rounds.
Version 1.1.4:
1 · Bullet speed increased by 5.
2 · Time between bullets being shot when Q is held down is reduced.
3 · Starting bullets reduced to 30.
4 · Interactive house temporarily removed.
5 · Fixed a bug where some bullets would speed up if a bullet hits a wall.
Version 1.2.0:
1 · Added 4 Difficulties
2 · Added a boss that spawns every 5 rounds
3 · Fixed a bug concerning direction of shooting bullets and the player's orientation
4 · Zombies have a chance to spawn with higher health.
5 · You can now press Enter to trade all coins for an item.
